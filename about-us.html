About CIAS Inc

Your Favourite Education Consultancy Service Provider

Our History

CIAS Inc was established in 2003 by Ms. Gursharan Kaur Kandra and was incorporated in 2010. Ms. Kandra, CEO and President of CIAS, is a renowned social worker who has been actively involved in international student recruitment since 2008. In the last five years we have introduced North American education to aspiring students in India and have expanded our services in other regions across the world, including Middle East, Nigeria, Singapore, UK and USA.

Overview

CIAS Inc is an agency dealing with recruitment of international students.CIAS Inc. was initiated to bring globalization of education, by providing students across the world with easy access to quality education from North American colleges and universities. We provide opportunities to study at some of the best universities and colleges in USA, Canada, UK and Europe.

We are not only a recruitment agency, but we also provide settlement services to international students with the help of Canadian Intercultural Association of Students (CIAOS) a non-profit organization, inclusive of post-landing services, job placements, business placements, and arrangement of co-op placements. We not only recruit students but we are also working on facilitating joint ventures. We also assist students in filling all kinds of forms i.e. extension of study permit, extension of work permit, extension of visa, multiple entry visa forms etc.

Mission

The Mission of CIAS is to provide outstanding education to students from variousemerging countries. In its endeavor to promote education, CIAS works towards bringing exceptional international education to the doorstep of students.

Vision

Our vision is promote and facilitate the globalization of education as well as to expand our services in markets demanding better quality education and meeting those needs by bringing world class, North American education doorstep prospective clients.

Goals & Strategies

  • To bring qualified and eligible students from across the world to live and study at colleges and universities in Canadian and USA
  • To continue representing our colleges and universities as reliable and reputable institutions that delivers quality education
  • To create a smoother transition for clients/student relocating to pursue their studies in North America
